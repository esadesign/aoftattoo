FAQ

Is it safe and hygienic?
Absolutely. I use 100% sterile, single-use disposable needles and follow strict professional hygiene standards to ensure your safety.

How do I book an appointment?
You can book a session by sending a message on Instagram or email. Please include your design idea, size, and placement.

What is the age limit?
You must be 18 years or older to get a tattoo. Please bring a valid ID (like a passport or driver's license) to your appointment.
